About Mail Header Analyzer

Digital forensic investigators need tools that will help them shift through large amount of data generated by email to save time, effort and obtain reliable results that will aid investigations.

A simple enriched user interface for parsing email headers and converting them to a human readable format.
Identification of hop delays, hop country and source of the email.
Results are retrieved and generated from the extracted email header less than minutes.
Present comprehensive result collated from the Internet that will be readily use as a digital evidence.
